French Fries Recipe Card

Ingredients for French Fries Recipe

Un Order List

  • Potatoes non starchy-waxy 5 large
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep-frying

Methods

Order List

  1. Peel the potatoes and keep them soaked in water. Cut them into one centimeter thick slices and further cut into finger sized pieces.

  2. Transfer the potato pieces into a zip-lock bag and seal after removing any excess air. Keep them in the deep freezer.

  3. When required remove them from the deep freezer and deep fry directly in medium hot oil till golden and crisp. Drain and place on absorbent paper
