Shani Dev is one of the most popular deities that the Hindus pray to ward off evil and remove obstacles. Suryadev (son of Brahma) and Sandna (the daughter of Daksha Prajapati) were Shri Shaneshwar's parents. Sandna and Suryadev had two sons Dakshinadhipati Yama and Shri Shaneshwar and four daughters Tapti, Bhadra, Kalindi and Savitri.
